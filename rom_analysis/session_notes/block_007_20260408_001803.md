### CONCLUSION: Growth formula CONFIRMED
  stat_at_level(L) = base + sum(random(1, growth_rate) for each level 2..L)
  This is equivalent to Pokemon-style random stat growth.
  The odd bytes (B1,B3,B5,B7) are MAX growth per level-up.
  Actual growth is random(1, max) — explaining the EV-like variance.
